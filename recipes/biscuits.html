Biscuits

Flaky, Fluffy, Fiery, whatever you want them to be!

This is my favorite biscuit recipe. My grandma always used to serve them up frozen for us kids to enjoy. I remember the smell like it was just 25 years ago. My sister never liked them as much as I did which is why I was always grandmas favorite.

You can serve these biscuits frozen or room temperature. Any toppings will do. Just throw in whatever's left over in the fridge. You'll love it and hubby who is a picky eater will too.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 lbs extra salted butter
  • the soundtrack to Secretary
  • 2 Tbsp baking powder
  • 3 cups half and half
  • hamburger leftovers

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Use noise cancelling headphones to drown out the sounds of your children playing while you listen to the soundtrack from Secretary.
  3. Mix all the ingredients together straight on to the parchment lined baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the edges are golden brown and the smell is insufferable.
  5. Slice into equal portions and freeze for later.
